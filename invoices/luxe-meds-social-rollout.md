# Luxe Meds — Social Media Product Launch Strategy

**Prepared:** 2026-03-31
**Platforms:** Facebook + Instagram
**Goal:** Convert existing followers into first customers via organic + paid rollout

---

## Phase 1: Warm-Up (Days 1–7)
**Goal:** Build curiosity without selling yet.

- **Teaser posts** (3–4 across the week)
  - "Something new is coming..." style content
  - Behind-the-scenes photos/videos (packaging, product shots, workspace)
  - Use Instagram Stories with countdown stickers and polls ("What do you think this is?")
- **Go Live** once — casual, unboxing-style or "here's what I've been working on"
- **Update bios** on both platforms — add tagline and link to product page
- **Pin a teaser post** to the top of the Facebook page

## Phase 2: Soft Launch (Days 8–14)
**Goal:** Introduce the product and drive first sales.

- **Official announcement post** — high-quality product photo + clear description of what it is, who it's for, and why it matters
- **Instagram Reel / Facebook Video** — short (30–60 sec) product demo or explainer
- **Stories daily** — rotate between:
  - Product features/benefits
  - Customer problem → your solution
  - Link to purchase (swipe up / link sticker)
- **Facebook post in relevant groups** (if applicable and allowed) — not spammy, value-first
- **Offer a launch incentive:**
  - Early-bird discount (first 50 orders)
  - Free shipping for launch week
  - Bundle deal

## Phase 3: Social Proof (Days 15–30)
**Goal:** Build trust and momentum.

- **Share testimonials/reviews** as they come in (screenshot DMs with permission, or ask for video reviews)
- **User-generated content** — encourage buyers to post and tag; repost their content
- **FAQ post or carousel** — address common questions
- **"Still available" reminder posts** — don't assume everyone saw the launch
- **Instagram Highlights** — create a dedicated highlight for the product (features, reviews, how-to)

## Phase 4: Paid Amplification (Days 15+)
**Goal:** Extend reach beyond organic followers.

- **Boost top-performing organic posts** — don't create new ads, amplify what's already working
- **Facebook/Instagram Ad targeting:**
  - Lookalike audience based on existing followers
  - Interest-based targeting for the product category
  - Retarget website visitors (install Meta Pixel on product pages if not already)
- **Budget suggestion:** Start small ($10–20/day), scale what converts
- **Square integration:** Ensure checkout is seamless from ad → product page → Square payment

## Ongoing Tactics
- **Post consistency:** 3–5x/week minimum across both platforms
- **Engage replies and DMs** — every comment is a potential sale
- **Monthly metrics review:** reach, engagement, link clicks, conversions
- **Seasonal/holiday tie-ins** when relevant
- **Email list:** Capture emails from buyers for future launches (Resend or similar)

## Tools & Integrations
- **Square** — payment processing (already in place)
- **Meta Business Suite** — schedule posts, manage ads, view analytics (free)
- **Meta Pixel** — install on product pages for ad retargeting
- **Canva** — quick graphics if needed
- **Later or Buffer** — optional post scheduling

---

*This strategy assumes an existing engaged following. Adjust pacing if follower engagement is low — may need a longer warm-up phase.*
